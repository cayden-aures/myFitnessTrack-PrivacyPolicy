Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3/17/26

myFitnessTrack ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is handled.

Information We Access

The app may access the following information on your device if you choose to grant permission:

  • Apple Health data, including:
    • walking and running distance
    • cycling distance
    • step count
    • workouts
    • heart rate
  • Photos you choose to add for shoes or bikes
  • App data you enter manually, such as:
    • shoe and bike details
    • workout logs
    • exercise notes
    • maintenance records
    • goals and preferences

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to provide app functionality, including:

  • tracking shoe mileage and wear
  • tracking bike mileage and maintenance
  • logging lifting workouts
  • estimating muscle recovery
  • importing and syncing fitness history from Apple Health
  • saving completed lifting workouts to Apple Health when you choose to do so
  • storing your preferences, such as units, goals, and display name

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple system storage.

The app does not operate its own servers and does not send your personal data to a developer-owned backend for storage or processing.

Third Parties

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external data collection tools.

If you choose to connect Apple Health, the app interacts with Apple's HealthKit framework according to Apple's platform rules and permissions.

Apple Health Data

Health data accessed through HealthKit is used only to provide health and fitness features inside the app.

HealthKit data is not used for advertising, marketing, or data brokering.

Your Control

You can control data access at any time by:

  • changing Health permissions in the iPhone Health app or Settings app
  • deleting app data from within the app, where supported
  • deleting the app from your device

If you revoke Health access, the app will no longer be able to read new Health data.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and the app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted at the current Privacy Policy URL.
