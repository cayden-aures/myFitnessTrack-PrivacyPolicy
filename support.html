What myFitnessTrack Helps You Track

myFitnessTrack is designed to help you manage:

  • shoe mileage and wear
  • bike mileage and maintenance
  • walking, running, and cycling history
  • lifting workouts and routines
  • exercise progress
  • muscle recovery
  • Apple Health imports and sync

Common Help Topics

You can contact support for help with:

  • Apple Health connection issues
  • missing or incorrect synced activity
  • shoe or bike assignment problems
  • lifting workout or exercise library issues
  • muscle recovery or progress tracking questions
  • app crashes or unexpected behavior
  • feature requests

Apple Health

If you use Apple Health with myFitnessTrack, make sure Health permissions are enabled for the data you want to import or save.

Health app → Sharing → Apps → myFitnessTrack

Privacy

myFitnessTrack stores your data locally on your device and uses Apple Health only with your permission.
